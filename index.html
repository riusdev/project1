The Challenge

Many cafés rely only on Instagram to present their business. Without a website, customers cannot easily explore the menu, atmosphere, or story behind the café. The challenge was to design a website that communicates warmth, comfort, and personality.

Designing with Human Emotion


The goal of this project was not only to display information, but to create a digital experience that feels warm and inviting. Through warm colors, spacious layouts, and visual storytelling, the website encourages visitors to imagine themselves enjoying coffee inside the café.
