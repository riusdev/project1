The Challenge

Many small cafés rely only on social media and lack a dedicated website to showcase their menu, atmosphere, and brand story. This project aims to create a visually appealing website that helps Café Luna attract more customers and communicate its cozy experience.

The Solution

The website focuses on creating an emotional connection with visitors by highlighting the atmosphere of the café, beautiful coffee presentation, and inviting visual storytelling. The design emphasizes simplicity, warm colors, and smooth user experience.
